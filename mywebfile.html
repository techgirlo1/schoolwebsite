About the University

With over 25,500 students, New castle University’s 10 colleges, schools and departments offer approximately 138 different degree programs at the bachelor’s, master’s and doctorate levels. These programs are offered on the University’s beautiful campuses in Statesboro, Savannah, Hinesville and through online instruction. We even have a study-abroad learning and research center in the charming city of Wexford, Ireland, marking New Castle as the only public U.S. university with a campus in the country.Whether a recent high school graduate, a military-affiliated student or an adult learner seeking to advance a career, New castle has a place to call home for everyone, ranking nationally in the Top 100 degree producers for diverse students.

With a single vision of growing others, our research is focused on community impact. Our teaching is focused on our students. And our students are focused on their future. New castle’s nationally accredited academic programs prepare graduates for the real world of career or academics from graduation day. A unit of the University System of New castle, the University boasts 300-plus student organizations, outstanding Division I athletics and state-of-the art residence halls and campus facilities.

The Office of Financial Aid is a unit of the Division of Enrollment Management.
